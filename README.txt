This is a java program to predict how internet customers usage will change over the year. 
The customer archetypes are randomly generated, and after each month they take a census of their 8 neighbors. 
There is a hierarchy of rules for how customers change based on their census. All customers make different amounts of profit for the internet company.
When main us run a neighborhood is randomly generated and simulates 12 months of customer changes and prints the annual revenue to the console.
